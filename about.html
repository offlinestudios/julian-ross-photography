ABOUT

Julian Ross Photography

Julian Ross is a Toronto-based photographer and studio owner with over seven years of experience behind the camera. His journey began with event photography, portraits, and lifestyle shoots, gradually expanding into motion capture and commercial work. Known for his attention to detail and ability to capture natural, expressive moments, Julian brings a high level of professionalism and creative direction to every session. Whether working with individuals or businesses, his focus is on delivering high-quality visuals that reflect personality, clarity, and purpose. Julian operates out of the studio he founded - Offline Studios, where he continues to build a reputation for accessible, efficient, and visually compelling photography and videography services.
